# Who is Liam Mccay?
 
Liam McCay is an 18 years old living in Ireland that is known as Sign Crushes Motorist, Moon Water, Birth day, Take Care, Hold, Manta, Miserable teens club, Makes his ribs show, Dead Calm. He is a music artist that has many alias. He has been actively releasing music. Duster plays a certain role in inspiring Liam to create his own slow-core musics. 

## Works that he has done

Moon water was his first ever album that contained 5 songs. It was released in 2021 with the title "Moon Water - EP" the album contains:

  

- Get out Of My Head

- This Place Is My Mind

- Spacey Interlude

- Sad Night Out

- Looking For The Door / Credits Song 

He stated that Moon Water was his worst project in his Instagram on September 2023. This laid the ground work on other projects by him in the following years.
